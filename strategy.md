# REDEVISE — MASTER STRATEGY DOCUMENT

**Positioning, Structure, Product Strategy & Execution Plan**

---

# 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Redevise is not a product company.

Redevise is an **Optimization Infrastructure Company** focused on transforming how individuals and organizations operate through intelligent systems.

The company builds interconnected tools powered by a shared intelligence layer (**Jarvis**) to:

* Reduce operational friction
* Improve decision-making
* Automate repetitive cognitive work
* Enhance human output

The long-term goal is to create a **compounding intelligence ecosystem** where every product strengthens the others.

---

# 2. CORE VISION

> “To redevise how people and organizations operate by embedding intelligence into everyday workflows.”

---

# 3. CORE PRINCIPLE

Every product must answer this:

**What process are we optimizing, and how dramatically are we improving it?**

If the improvement isn’t significant → the product doesn’t belong.

---

# 4. COMPANY STRUCTURE

## 4.1 Parent Company: Redevise

**Responsibilities:**

* Strategic direction
* Product philosophy enforcement
* Shared infrastructure (AI, data, APIs)
* Branding and positioning
* Intelligence layer (Jarvis)

---

## 4.2 Division A: Business Optimization (B2B)

**Primary Revenue Driver**

### Products:

#### 1. SupporTribe

* Customer support intelligence platform
* Focus: agent performance, conversation quality, workflow clarity
* Differentiator: agent-first + AI-assisted decision making

#### 2. Eduplugg

* Modular school operations system
* Focus: simplifying administration, communication, and academic workflows

#### 3. Jarvis (Core Engine)

* Internal intelligence system powering all products
* Handles:

  * Pattern recognition
  * Insights
  * Automation triggers
  * Personalization

**Strategic Role:**
Jarvis is the backbone of the entire company.
It must not be treated as a side product.

---

## 4.3 Division B: Consumer Optimization (B2C)

**Purpose: Distribution + Data Flywheel**

### Products:

#### 1. StreakMonkey

* Habit formation and behavioral consistency tool
* Focus: discipline, routine optimization

#### 2. CitationPro

* Research productivity browser extension
* Focus: reducing friction in academic and professional writing

**Strategic Role:**

* Acquire users at scale
* Gather behavioral insights (ethically)
* Feed intelligence into Jarvis
* Build brand familiarity

---

## 4.4 Division C: Specialized Vertical Tools

### Product:

#### Ascribe

* Church presentation software

**Strategic Options:**

* Go deep into church ecosystem and dominate
* OR maintain as a lean, stable product

**Warning:**
A floating niche product without a clear strategy becomes a distraction.

# 5. THE CORE ADVANTAGE — JARVIS

## 5.1 Definition

Jarvis is the **intelligence layer** that connects all Redevise products.

It is not just an API.

It is:

* A learning system
* A context engine
* A decision-support layer

---

## 5.2 Capabilities

* Cross-product data understanding
* Behavioral pattern recognition
* Context-aware recommendations
* Workflow automation triggers
* Personalization per user and per organization

---

## 5.3 Example Flywheel

* SupporTribe identifies customer frustration trends
* Jarvis processes patterns
* Eduplugg improves communication workflows
* StreakMonkey applies behavioral nudges
* Future systems become smarter instantly

---

## 5.4 Outcome

Redevise becomes:

> A system that gets smarter every time any user interacts with any product.

That is the moat.
